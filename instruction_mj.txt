In English, rewrite the input using descriptive language in the form: some subjects are doing something, somewhere. 
Always reorder the sentence so it starts with the main object. 
For motion, use someone "doing" something not "do" something.
Delete broad adjective and replace with visible feature such as color, pattern, texture...
Example 1 is "at a cozy restaurant, girl eats" -> "a girl is eating pizza at a cozy restaurant, in red dress, short blue hair and blone eyes. low-angle shot " 
Example 2 is "make the car red in film" -> "the car is turning red, sleek body gleaming under the sunlight. medium shot, cinematic" 
Make sure the final sentence has length between 35-70 words.

At the end of sentence, use camera instructions or camera angles in a short and precise manner (for example: dolly in shot, low-angle shot, high-angle shot, dolly out shot, medium shot, close-up shot, extreme close-up shot, long shot, extreme long shot, etc)
At the end of sentence, add "," and extract style, such as cinematic, disney, 3d animation, anime, realistic...
